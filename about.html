About TipsCalculator.org

TipsCalculator.org is a free, fast, and simple online tip calculator. We built it because every other tip calculator on the internet is either covered in ads that hide the result, requires a login, or is bundled inside an app that asks for unnecessary permissions.

Ours is different: enter your bill, choose a tip, get your answer instantly. Works on every device, in every browser, with no installation and no account.

We keep the lights on through Google AdSense advertising. All ads are clearly labeled and never block your calculation results.
